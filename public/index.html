vPDF validation

Block.co, a pioneer in Blockchain Credentialing applications, offers an open source platform to issue and verify digital documents in a completely decentralized way; i.e. with no dependencies to the issuing institution or anyone else other than Bitcoin's blockchain. This site is a front-end of the open source verification software that anyone can use to validate digital documents. vPDF documents are issued as PDF files with special that anchor them into the blockchain.

Please note that the actual PDF document itself is not published to the blockchain, but rather a fingerprint of the PDF, which is enough to validate that the exact document was indeed published by the issuer and timestamped by Bitcoinâ€™s blockchain.
